About Samir Metal & Tubes

34+ Years of Excellence in Steel Industry

1991

Foundation

Samir Metal & Tubes was established, initially dealing in non-ferrous tubes and building our foundation in the metal industry.

1994

Expansion

Diversified operations and started importing seamless pipes and tubes of ferrous materials, marking our entry into the global supply chain.

2006

First Warehouse at Taloja

Established our first warehouse in Taloja to enhance storage capacity and distribution efficiency.

2014

Branch Office

Started a new branch office in Ahmedabad for strategic expansion at key locations.

Now

Market Leadership

Achieved major market share by consistently providing quality products and reliable service to industrial clients across India.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality seamless pipes and tubes through reliable import operations, maintaining extensive stock to serve our clients promptly while ensuring quality standards and timely delivery.

Our Vision

To be India's most trusted partner in the steel industry, known for our comprehensive product range, quality assurance, and exceptional customer service in the B2B industrial sector.

Operational Excellence

Regular Import Schedule

Our import operations run on a regular basis, ensuring fresh inventory and consistent quality. We maintain strategic relationships with global suppliers to guarantee reliable supply chains.

Comprehensive Stock Management

We maintain stock in a wide range of sizes and various grades at any given time, enabling us to serve clients promptly. Our inventory management system ensures optimal stock levels.

Indian Mill Partnerships

Besides imported materials, we stock products from leading Indian mills including Maharashtra Seamless Ltd. and The Indian Seamless Metal & Tubes Ltd.

Modern Infrastructure

Our fully facilitated warehouse is equipped with modern storage and handling equipment, supported by in-house software packages for procurement, planning, and inventory control.
