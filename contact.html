Frequently Asked Questions

What is your minimum order quantity?

We can supply materials in small quantities as well as large bulk orders. Please contact us with your specific requirements for accurate pricing and availability.

Do you provide Mill Test Certificates?

Yes, we provide original Mill Test Certificates (EN 10204/DIN 50049 3.1.B) and IBR certifications with all our materials.

What are your delivery timeframes?

For stock items, we can deliver within 2-7 days depending on location. For indent items, delivery time varies from 4-12 weeks based on specifications.

Do you provide third-party inspection?

Yes, our materials are covered under internationally renowned inspection agencies like Intertek, Bureau Veritas, DNV, SGS, and Lloyd's.

Can you supply custom specifications?

Yes, we can supply materials as per customer specifications and international standards. Please share your detailed requirements for a customized quote.

Do you provide technical support?

Our experienced technical team provides complete support for material selection, specifications, and applications to ensure you get the right product for your needs.
